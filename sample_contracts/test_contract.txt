RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT
1. PARTIES AND PREMISES
This Agreement is entered into between Apex Realty Holdings ("Landlord") and the undersigned ("Tenant") for the premises located at 742 Evergreen Terrace, Apt 4B.

2. TERM AND AUTOMATIC RENEWAL
The lease shall begin on August 1, 2026, and terminate on July 31, 2027. Upon expiration, this lease shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms at a mandatory 25% rent increase unless the Tenant provides written notice via certified mail exactly ninety-five (95) days prior to the expiration date. Notice provided at ninety-four (94) days or earlier than one hundred (100) days shall be deemed invalid.

3. RIGHT OF ENTRY AND SURVEILLANCE
The Landlord reserves the absolute right to enter the premises at any hour of the day or night, without prior notice to the Tenant, for the purposes of inspection, repair, or showing the property to prospective tenants or buyers. The Tenant agrees that the Landlord may maintain security cameras in common areas, including the interior living room and hallways, to ensure property preservation, and Tenant waives any right to privacy regarding these interior feeds.

4. MAINTENANCE, REPAIRS, AND AS-IS CONDITION
The Tenant accepts the premises in its absolute "as-is" condition. The Tenant explicitly waives the implied warranty of habitability. The Tenant shall be solely responsible for all maintenance and repairs, including but not limited to HVAC failure, plumbing blockages, structural roof leaks, mold remediation, and pest control, regardless of the cause or origin of the defect. Failure to repair any defect within 48 hours constitutes a material breach of this lease.

5. LIABILITY WAIVER AND INDEMNIFICATION
The Landlord, its agents, and employees shall not be liable for any injury, death, or property damage occurring on the premises, even if caused by the active negligence, gross negligence, or intentional misconduct of the Landlord or due to the total failure of structural elements of the building. The Tenant agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Landlord from all claims arising from any occurrence on the property.

6. SEIZURE OF PERSONAL PROPERTY (LANDLORD'S LIEN)
If rent is unpaid for more than three (3) days past the due date, the Landlord shall have the right, without legal process or court order, to enter the premises, change the locks, and seize any and all personal property belonging to the Tenant (including electronics, clothing, and documents) to sell at private auction to satisfy the debt.

7. WAIVER OF JURY TRIAL AND LEGAL FEES
In the event of any legal dispute arising from this agreement, the Tenant explicitly waives the right to a trial by jury and waives the right to join any class-action lawsuit. In all legal actions, the Tenant shall pay all of the Landlord’s attorney fees and court costs, regardless of which party prevails in the dispute.

8. LIQUIDATED DAMAGES AND UNILATERAL TERMINATION
Should the Tenant breach any minor provision of this lease (including, but not limited to, leaving a cardboard box on the balcony or playing music audible outside the room after 9:00 PM), the Landlord may immediately terminate this lease, evict the Tenant within 24 hours, and retain the entire security deposit plus an additional $5,000 as liquidated damages. The Landlord retains the right to terminate this lease at any time, for any reason or no reason, upon 48 hours' written notice to the Tenant, without penalty.
