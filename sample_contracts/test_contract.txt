RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT
1. PARTIES AND PREMISES
This Agreement is entered into between Apex Realty Holdings ("Landlord") and the undersigned ("Tenant") for the premises located at 742 Evergreen Terrace, Apt 4B.

2. TERM AND AUTOMATIC RENEWAL
The lease shall begin on August 1, 2026, and terminate on July 31, 2027. Upon expiration, this lease shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms at a mandatory 25% rent increase unless the Tenant provides written notice via certified mail exactly ninety-five (95) days prior to the expiration date. Notice provided at ninety-four (94) days or earlier than one hundred (100) days shall be deemed invalid.

3. RIGHT OF ENTRY AND SURVEILLANCE
The Landlord reserves the absolute right to enter the premises at any hour of the day or night, without prior notice to the Tenant, for the purposes of inspection, repair, or showing the property to prospective tenants or buyers. The Tenant agrees that the Landlord may maintain security cameras in common areas, including the interior living room and hallways, to ensure property preservation, and Tenant waives any right to privacy regarding these interior feeds.

4. MAINTENANCE, REPAIRS, AND AS-IS CONDITION
The Tenant accepts the premises in its absolute "as-is" condition. The Tenant explicitly waives the implied warranty of habitability. The Tenant shall be solely responsible for all maintenance and repairs, including but not limited to HVAC failure, plumbing blockages, structural roof leaks, mold remediation, and pest control, regardless of the cause or origin of the defect. Failure to repair any defect within 48 hours constitutes a material breach of this lease.
